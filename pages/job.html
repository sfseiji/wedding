Job Opportunities

If you are interested in joining our research group, please consider the following opportunities. For further details or inquiries, feel free to contact me.

Postdoctoral Positions

Postdoc opportunities will be announced, probably once every few years at the AAS job register. However, availability depends on the funding situation.
Please feel free to contact me if you are interested or have any questions.

Dunlap Fellow & CITA Fellow

There are several anual prize fellowship opportunities in Toronto. If your research interests are synergistic with mine, you may consider applying for the Dunlap Fellow program, which I can be your host.
While direct hosting is not available, collaborations with CITA Fellow are very welcome. Please reach out to me to get in touch for further discussion.

PhD Students

Aspiring PhD students should apply to the Department of Astronomy at the University of Toronto. After admission, you will participate in a first-year project (AST1501 or AST1500). Upon successful matching, you will have the opportunity to join my research group.

Undergraduate Students

In principle, I do not routinely accept undergraduate students. However, depending on circumstances, there may be occasional opportunities. For instance, the Dunlap Observatory Summer School (link) sometimes offers positions.

Visiting Students

I can also host visiting students for periods ranging from a few months to one year. If you are interested in visiting, please feel free to contact me for further discussion.
